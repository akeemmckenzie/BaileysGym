Frequently Asked Questions

Are my Family and Friends allowed to use the key card?

Unfortunately, this is against club policy. Your key card is uniquely yours.

What is the minimum age requirement to join Bailey's Health & Fitness

The minimum age requirement at Bailey's is 15 years old with parental consent.

Are guests allowed at the club?

If you are going with a current member, they are allowed a certain amount of guests per month depending on their membership. However, we also offer a daily rate of $10 for visitors.

May I bring children into the gym while I workout?

Yes, select locations have Children Play Area for children between 12 months to 12 years old.

Can I use other Bailey's Gym locations?

If you have the Platinum membership type, you have access to all Bailey's gym locations.

Is Bailey's Gym open 24 hours?

All locations excluding Atlantic, and Deerwood are open 24 hours.

How long does it take to sign up for a membership?

Applying for a membership can take anywhere from 5-10 minutes.

Does Bailey's Health & Fitness offer Personal Training?

Yes we have an excellent team at Sweat PT, who are there to meet all your fitness needs.

Is Bailey's Health & Fitness open for the Holiday's?

Depending on which holiday, the location may be open for limited hours. However, many locations are open 24 hours for memebers to use.
