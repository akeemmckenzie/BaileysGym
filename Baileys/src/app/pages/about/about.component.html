Business Background

Don Bailey, the 1979 Terry Parker high school graduate founded the first Bailey’s Health & Fitness. Bailey received a football scholarship for the University of Tennessee, after his attendance he went on to play at the University of Central Florida. After a football injury in 1981, he was no longer able to play on the field. Don Bailey’s dream was to become a gym owner. He opened his first-ever gym after moving back to Jacksonville, FL, however, it started as a small indoor space for his friends to workout in while he attended welding school to build his own gym equipment. After a few years, his younger brother Darryl Bailey partnered in 1989, and two years later the youngest brother joined, David Bailey. Today, fifteen Bailey’s Health and Fitness gyms are open in Jacksonville, FL.



Mission

Bailey’s commitment is to ensure everyone is taken care of and create a ‘feel safe’ zone. We reassure all guests to feel encouraged by the atmosphere and bring out their best in their workout. No Judgement. Bailey’s Commitment.
